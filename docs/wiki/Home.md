# Welcome to Sunbrim Premier Wiki

Sunbrim Premier is a specialized ecommerce platform for the bread and bakery confectionery industry. It is designed to be lightweight, mobile-first, and highly efficient for both customers and administrators.

## Project Vision
To provide local bakeries with a professional digital presence and a streamlined "Quick Order" system that works reliably on mobile devices and low-bandwidth connections.
